# Privacy Policy for Property Compass

**Last Updated: November 17, 2025**

Property Compass ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Search Information
- Property addresses you search for
- Geographic coordinates (latitude/longitude) of searched properties
- Calculated property directions and bearings
- Search timestamps

### 1.2 Device Information
- Operating system type and version (iOS/Android)
- Device timezone and UTC offset
- Device locale and region (e.g., "US", "GB")
- App version

### 1.3 Usage Analytics
- App interactions (button taps, screen views)
- Feature usage patterns
- Error logs and app crashes
- Session duration and app lifecycle events

### 1.4 Local Data
- Recent search history stored on your device
- App preferences and settings

---

## 2. Information We DO NOT Collect

- We do NOT require user accounts or authentication
- We do NOT collect your name, email, or phone number
- We do NOT track your real-time location or GPS coordinates
- We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
- We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 To Provide Services
- Calculate and display property directions
- Show nearby places and cities
- Cache your searches for quick access
- Display search history

### 3.2 To Improve the App
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Understand which features are most used
- Optimize app performance

### 3.3 To Manage Resources
- Enforce daily search limits to prevent abuse
- Monitor API usage and costs

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Cloud Storage
- Search data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore
- Analytics data is stored in Firebase
- All data transmissions are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS

### 4.2 Local Storage
- Recent searches are cached locally on your device
- Local cache expires after 3 days
- You can clear cached data through your device settings

### 4.3 Data Retention
- Search history is retained for analytics purposes
- Analytics data is retained for up to 14 months
- Local cached data expires after 3 days

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general property research and does not target children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, please contact us.

---

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

### 8.1 Access and Control
- View your saved searches in the app
- Delete individual saved searches
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data

### 8.2 Data Deletion Requests
- Contact us to request deletion of your cloud-stored search data
- We will respond to deletion requests within 30 days

### 8.3 Opt-Out
- You can stop data collection by uninstalling the app
- Clearing app data will remove local caches
