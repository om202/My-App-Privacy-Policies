# Privacy Policy — Nepali Keyboard

**Last Updated: March 19, 2026**

Noble Stack ("we", "our", "us") built the **Nepali Keyboard** app as a free utility for macOS. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.**

Nepali Keyboard performs all transliteration entirely on your device using an offline engine. No text you type, no keystrokes, and no personal information of any kind is ever transmitted, logged, stored, or shared with us or any third party.

## No Network Access

Nepali Keyboard does not connect to the internet. The app:

- Does **not** make any network requests
- Does **not** use analytics or tracking services
- Does **not** contain any third-party SDKs
- Does **not** include advertising

## No Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or process:

- Keystrokes or text input
- Personal identifiers (name, email, phone)
- Device information
- Usage data or analytics
- Location data
- Cookies or similar technologies

## Data Storage

All transliteration happens in real time within the app's memory. No data is written to disk, no logs are saved, and no information persists between sessions.

## App Sandbox

Nepali Keyboard runs inside the macOS App Sandbox with minimal permissions. It only requires the Input Method Kit entitlement necessary to function as a keyboard input source.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
