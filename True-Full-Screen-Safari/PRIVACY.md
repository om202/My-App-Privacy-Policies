# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 22, 2026
**App:** True Full Screen for Safari

## Summary

True Full Screen does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. The app works entirely on your Mac and never sends information to the developer or any third party.

## What the app stores, and where

The only data the app writes anywhere is your own preferences:

- Your zoom level
- Your chosen scaling strategy
- Whether the extension is enabled

These values are saved using Safari's built-in local extension storage (`browser.storage.local`). They never leave your device. Uninstalling the extension removes them.

## What the app does not do

- **No analytics.** No usage tracking, event logging, or telemetry of any kind.
- **No network requests.** The extension makes no calls to any server operated by the developer or any third party.
- **No advertising.** No ads, no ad SDKs, no ad identifiers.
- **No cookies or fingerprinting.** The extension does not set cookies, read tracking IDs, or fingerprint your device.
- **No access to your browsing history.** The extension activates only on Netflix and YouTube video pages, and only reads the structure of the page it is acting on to apply zoom.
- **No third parties.** The app integrates with no external services, SDKs, or partners.

## What Apple collects

Downloads, refunds, and purchase details are handled by Apple under Apple's own privacy policy — not by this app. If you have questions about App Store data, see [apple.com/privacy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. It is rated 4+.

## Changes to this policy

If the privacy practices ever change in a future version, this document will be updated and the effective date at the top revised. Since the app collects nothing today, any change would need to be a meaningful addition — not fine print.
